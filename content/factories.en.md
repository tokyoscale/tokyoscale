## Instrumentation partners

For water, power, and flow measurement on the plant floor:

- **Ningbo Water Meter** — bulk ultrasonic meters, AMI/AMR, remote monitoring of abnormal usage for utilities and HSE teams
- **Shandong Rongxian Instrument** — electromagnetic flow meters, prepaid three-phase electricity meters, combined flow and power monitoring on process lines
- **Ningbo Xingyuan Meter** — single/multi-jet smart meters, M-Bus, industrial meter bodies and fittings
- **Yuhuan Sierjia Valve** — motorized valves, remote shutoff, process-line flow control

## Themes we emphasize

- **IoT / sensors / edge** — vibration, temperature, flow, consumption: start from **definitions of abnormal** and **escalation paths**, not sensor catalogs.
- **Water, energy, material loss** — smart metering for anomaly usage and leakage candidates—often landing well with ops, HSE, and finance together.
- **Offshore peers & contract factories** — sync **purpose of visibility** with **who can act** when habits and systems diverge.
- **Beyond vanity OEE** — mean time to recover, repeat incidents, queue time—signals that steer daily work.
